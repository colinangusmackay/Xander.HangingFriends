Help

To start with select the blank tile on the bottom row, then click the blue placeholder representing the last letter of the word. The answer line will then place blank tiles to that point.

If you make a mistake, just click placeholders futher right to add more tiles, or click the last blank tile on the answer line to remove that tile.

To enter a guess click the letter tile corresponding to the guess. That tile will go blue and the other tiles will dim. If that guess was correct you can place it in the answer line by pressing the corresponding tile int he answer line. If you make a mistake you can press the tile again to blank it.

To see a list of possible words press the magnifying glass tile on the bottom right of the grid. Possible answers will appear below. If there are more than 200 answers only the first 200 are shown.
